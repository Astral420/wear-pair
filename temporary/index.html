Fashion Tips

A white top is a versatile choice that can be paired with almost any bottoms. Consider augmenting your look with a colorful necklace or a statement belt to add a personal touch.

Choose a top that complements the color of your white top. For instance, light blue or coral are great options that provide a fresh contrast.

Accessorize strategically. A pair of statement earrings can elevate your outfit, while a sleek bracelet adds a touch of elegance.
